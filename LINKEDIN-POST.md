## When to post

Tuesday or Wednesday, 8-10am your timezone for B2B GTM audience. Avoid Friday afternoon and weekends.

## What to do in the first 60 minutes

- Reply to the first 5 comments within 10 minutes — algo weighs early engagement heavily
- DM 3 people you know personally who are in GTM + technical enough to install, ask for honest feedback
- Don't repost the link in your own comments (LinkedIn deprioritizes external links in comments)

## After the post

- Update the repo README to embed the demo GIF if it isn't there yet
- Watch GitHub stars/forks for the first 48 hours
- If anyone files an issue, respond same day — open-source social proof compounds fast
- A week later, post a "what I learned shipping" follow-up. The second post often outperforms the first because the audience is warmer.
